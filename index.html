About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate advised by Prof. Chongjie Zhang , at Institute for Interdisciplinary Information Sciences (IIIS), Tsinghua University, headed by Prof. Andrew Yao. My research interests include Reinforcement Learning and Deep Learning. My main goal is to improve the sample-efficiency of reinforcement learning via efficient representation learning, episodic control, and model-based approaches.
